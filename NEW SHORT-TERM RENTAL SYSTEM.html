SHORT-TERM RENTAL PHOTOS

I once had a short-term rental experience for taking a course in Beijing ftheor two month. I still remember my difficulties through the whole process from finding information to seeing the house to finally get all things done. It is really complicated to find a suitable room and settle down everything. There are multiple reasons for people to have short-term rental.

DEFINITION

HORT-TERM RENTAL is renting a house or a room for a short period(usually less than 6 months). People have short-term rental mainly because they need to go to another city for studying, working, travelling or family affairs. There are many options of short-term rental: Single Room，Double Room，King Size & Queen Size Room，Standard，Triple，Quad，Suite，Apartment or Villa. The choice depend on individuals considering different living requirements.
Then, why most people choose short-term rental other than friends’ home, hotel or youth hostel ? So, I want to design an innovative, affordable, movable and convenient short-term rental service system.

INTERVIEWS

I have interviewed 8 people of their renting experience and how they think of their renting process. I find as college students do not have incomes, price are the most consideration. However, young people are willing to pay for convenience or privacy because they want to have a high-quality life.

QUESTIONS TO ASK

These questions are listed in four catagories: Before internship, On internship, After internship and Feelings. By asking these questions, i could gather their basic information and conclude some similaries and differences from their specific short-term rental experience.
