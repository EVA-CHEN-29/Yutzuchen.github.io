INTRODUCTION

1. Many countries around the world are facing severe aging social problems. China's aging process is the world's number one. By 2020, China's aging population will reach 355 million. In 2050, it will reach 437 million, accounting for more than 30% of the total population.

2. At present, there are more than 62,000 old-age universities and senior schools at all levels in the country, with more than 8 million students and more than 5 million students participating in distance learning.

3. For the retired senior learners, they have more free time and may lose the focus of work and social interaction.They may feel life has no sense of purpose, loneliness, emptiness, and easy to cause various mental illnesses.

DESIGN THINKING

I want to make a display system for senior learners in the elderly schools.
This display system enables senior learners to have more interations through work sharing. They can use to find suitable classes or chat with their classmates and teachers in or out of the class.








STORYBOARD

This shows how to use display system. We will know how this app changes Mrs Shen’s life in and out elderly school and how she feels.

FLOWCHART

This app aims to create a platform for the elderly to feel connected in the classroom by sharing works with each other. The senior learners could choose their classroom first and then see works that related to a specific interest. They can also search and apply to join in the specific classroom to upload their class works or performance pictures. In the classroom homepage, the elderly can socialize with their classmates or those who share the same interest in the way of comments, click likes or add to favorite. They can also manage their own single work page and see others’ as well.
