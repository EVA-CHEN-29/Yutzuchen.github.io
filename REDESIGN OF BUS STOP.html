BACKGROUND

BUS SYSTEM IN WUHAN

Wuhan is an emerging city with an urban area of ​​8,589.15 square kilometers and a total population of 11.081 million. So there is a very complicated and huge bus system: 8800 bus stops in total with 80% of coverage and 587 bus lines with 72% of coverage.

OBSERVATION

I choose two typical sites around Wuhan university: The first one is South East Lake Road Bus Stop. It is on a narroaw road by the East Lake and the main passengers are visitors and students; The second one is Cha Gang Road Bus Stop. It is beside the residential area and often crowded with 8 buses lines going through. The main passengers are residents and students.

A form is designed to write down details of observations such as Location, Date, Type Of Passengers, Timeline and Walkng Routes. These photos below show some of the observation forms from two bus stops.

INTERVIEWS

Through observation, i got data of passengers’ action and the specific time period of every action they took. Then, interviews were taken to gather information of how people think about bus stop and why they take each action. Besides, how they choose for every move or decisions matters a lot. In order to more deeply understand people there, i asked two types of questions first and classified my interviewees into groups to see whether there are some similarities or disparaties between them.
